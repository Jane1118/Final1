Rcommendation

Good to know

HOTPOT 100% needs to try, especially the fresh squid. The flavor Vietnams white wine is impressive and very hot, worth trying.

This restaurant is always very busy in an rush hour. I think a proper time for lunch might be after 2:00 pm, and for dinner might be before 6:00pm. I don't think the juice here is as good as its hotpot cause there are many super good small cafes and smoothies shops to exlpore.

Since many russians live here, the menu is written by Vietnamese, English and Russian. Waiters here can speak fluent English, Russian and even Spanish!! Some of them are very handsome!! They accept both USD and Vitenam Dong, but USD might be a little bit more expensive cause restaurants in Vietnam tend to use a higher currency exchange rate.
